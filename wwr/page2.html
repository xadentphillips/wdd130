Rates and Scheduling

June

We are open Monday through Saturday throughout the entire month of June. June rates start at $45 a person with a 20% discount when tickets are purchased in groups of six or above. (Not Compatible with any other offers)

July

We are open Monday through Saturday every day in July except for the 4th. We will only offer our shorter tours on the 4th and close at 1:00pm. July rates start at $55 a person with a 20% discout when tickets are purchased in groups of six or above. (Not Compatible with any other offers)

August

We are open Monday through Friday for the first three full weeks of August. August rates start at $40 a person with a 20% discount when tickets are purchased in groups of four or above. (Not Compatible with any other offers)
