Overview

Purpose

We hope to spread the exhilaration of white water rafting to as many individuals as we can reach. We hope to get people off of screens and into nature.

Audience

We want to help teenagers and young adults to experience nature. They spend so much time in virtual worlds, we would like to fulfill their need for adventure by giving them a great experience in the real world.
